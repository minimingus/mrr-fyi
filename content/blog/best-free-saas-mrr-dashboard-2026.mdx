Every SaaS founder needs to track MRR. But most MRR dashboard tools are priced for funded startups — not for indie hackers building their first $1K MRR product. Here's a comparison of the best free options in 2026.

## What you need from an MRR dashboard (at early stage)

Before comparing tools, be clear about what actually matters when you're below $10K MRR:

- **Accurate MRR calculation** — connected to your payment processor, not manually entered
- **Month-over-month trend** — is revenue growing, flat, or declining?
- **Churn visibility** — how much MRR are you losing each month?
- **No credit card, no friction** — you're not going to set up a tool with a 14-day trial and a sales call

Everything else (cohort analysis, LTV prediction, revenue forecasting) is noise until you're scaling a known-good business model.

## The main contenders

### Baremetrics

**Free tier**: Yes — free up to $1K MRR (self-reported)

Baremetrics is the gold standard for subscription analytics. Stripe and Paddle integration, clean charts, MRR/ARR/LTV/churn in one view. Their "Pulse" daily email is genuinely useful.

**The catch**: Free tier requires manual data entry or is very limited on integrations. Real-time Stripe sync requires a paid plan ($108/mo). For early-stage founders, you'll outgrow the free tier before you need the paid features.

### ChartMogul

**Free tier**: Yes — free up to $10K MRR

ChartMogul's free tier is the most generous of the traditional analytics tools. Full Stripe/Braintree/Recurly integration, cohort analysis, customer-level data. It's built for B2B SaaS but works fine for indie products.

**The catch**: The UI is complex. Setup takes time. If you're doing $500 MRR with 20 customers, ChartMogul is more dashboard than you need. It shines at $10K+, not at $1K.

### Profitwell (now Paddle Metrics)

**Free tier**: Yes — free forever for Stripe users

Profitwell was always the free option — their business model was data aggregation, not subscriptions. After the Paddle acquisition, the free analytics layer still works for Stripe users.

**The catch**: The product has stagnated since the acquisition. Some features are being deprecated or moved to paid Paddle plans. Not recommended for new setups in 2026.

### mrr.fyi

**Free tier**: Yes — free public profile with Stripe verification, forever

mrr.fyi is different from the others. It's not just a private analytics dashboard — it's a public MRR leaderboard for indie hackers and bootstrapped founders. Free accounts get:

- Stripe Connect integration (read-only, pulls verified MRR automatically)

There's no chart of your cohort churn over the last 18 months. What you get instead is a clean public number — verified by Stripe — that builds credibility with your audience, customers, and potential co-founders.

**The catch**: It's not a full analytics suite. If you want cohort retention curves and LTV distributions, use ChartMogul. If you want your MRR verified and public with zero maintenance cost, mrr.fyi is the answer.

## Why free matters

MRR tracking tools charge you a percentage of your MRR. At 1% of $500/mo, that's $5/mo — fine. At 1% of $50K/mo, it's $500/mo and now your analytics bill is a real cost.

Starting free, with a tool that stays free as you grow, removes one more barrier between you and launching. That's worth optimizing for early on.
